Confidentiality Policy

George Fisher is committed to safeguarding the confidential information of his clients. All personal information is held in the strictest confidence. I consider it a privilege to work with my clients to help them meet their financial goals. I recognize that this is a privilege that comes with responsibility – a responsibility to maintain the confidentiality of their personal information. I take this responsibility very seriously.

To conduct regular business, I may collect nonpublic personal information from sources such as applications, statements, tax or other forms provided by clients or information about their transactions with other firms. I share nonpublic information solely to provide client service and I do not disclose any nonpublic personal information about clients or former clients to anyone, except as required by law.

At times, I may disclose nonpublic personal information to nonaffiliated third parties. I may disclose nonpublic personal information about a client to the following types of non-affiliated third parties: money management institutions in order to maintain clients’ accounts; financial service providers such as mortgage brokers, insurance companies, banks, attorneys, accountants, and actuary firms (but only with a client’s prior authorization); or service providers that assist me with information systems management, account reconciliation and report production services, and secure document destruction, retention, and storage to facilitate our normal business operations.

Internet cookies are a feature of web browser software that can gather information about website visitors and allows web servers to recognize the computer used to access a website. I use the StatCounter website visitor counter service which uses cookies to record visits but no personal information is maintained or used in any way.

I am committed to maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and security of the personal information that is entrusted to me by my clients. Except as described above, I will not share client information. However, if information is requested by subpoena or investigation of fraudulent activity, etc. I may be required by law to comply. I will not rent, sell, trade, or otherwise release or disclose any personal information about clients. Any information that my clients provide to me is for professional use only. If clients decide to terminate their agreements, I will continue to adhere to these privacy policies and practices.
