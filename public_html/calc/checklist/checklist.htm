JPJ Trading Checklist

► Remember that you usually only want the trade the first time the setup occurs

► As you’re finishing one trade, be looking for the next setup

► Singles repel; Dbl TPO hi/lo attract

► Stops after the market goes by instead of limits resting can save a stop-out if the market doesn't reverse

► Don’t stay past 1:00PM (end of J period)

Clean Execution

  • Left-click Limit, right-click Stop
  • Limit Buy below (Blue), Limit Sell above (Red)

Pre Market

  1. Set MP Time Template to 24 Hour
  2. Set up EFS functions
    1. Line and Labels
      1. overnight high & low
      2. previous day's high + 2.5/low - 2.5
    2. Opening Range and VA Entry … previous day’s VAH & VAL (must be a 1-minute chart)
    3. Weekly Pivots
    4. MP Periods
  3. Find out if there’s any news
  4. Anticipate setups for opening In or Out Of Value based on overnight trading

At the Open

  1. Open IN (or very close to) Value
    1. Look to remain in Value: short at VAH and/or a long at VAL

  2. Open OUT OF Value
    1. If 3 or more points out of Value, look for an Opening Range Trade back toward Value: long below or-l or short above or-h
    2. ... if opening closer than 3 points out of Value, look to short the VAL or buy the VAH
       
    3. Anticipate long at Low - 2.5 or short at High + 2.5

At 9:55 AM … NEWS

  1. If there’s NEWS, get off your trades
  2. Set MP Time Template to Future
  3. Turn on EFS functions
    1. Daily HiLoPPB (toggle PPB lines)
    2. Initial Balance
    3. Daily Pivot
    4. Range Extension (requires daily hi/lo and IB)
  4. Plan post-IB setups

At 10:30AM … IB Finished

  1. With a Wide IB (7 or above)
    1. trade the rotation (short dVAH, long dVAL) if it’s available around G period: early
    2. If you opened IN Value,  and trade back and forth through the OR,
        look for the 1-tick_IB trade to move back into the IB
    3. Look for 50% IB trade

  1. With a Narrow IB
    1. If you opened OUT of Value, look for the 1-tick_IB trade to continue in RE direction (use a STOP to catch)
      ► this has the risk of leaving you the wrong way at the high or low of the day
    2. Look for Dbl IB trade reversal early in the day: sell the Dbl IB High, buy the Dbl IB Low;
      after 3:00 PM if outside the Dbl IB, look for a reversal the other way: buy the Dbl IB High, sell the Dbl IB Low

  2. Anticipate long at Low - 2.5 or short at High + 2.5 and the Dentist numbers

  3. If you opened OUT of Value and trade back into Value for two consecutive brackets (one finishes, the next starts), it may fill the whole Value Area (80% rule), look for rotational trade

  4. Watch for Range Extension followed by pullback:
    1. Short the dVAH from below
    2. Long the dVAL from above

  5. If the Developing Value trade gets stopped out, or if there's a second pullback,
    watch for Symmetrical Neutral (equal RE on both sides)
    1. Anticipate a move back toward the IB after the symmetrical RE develops
      (use a STOP to catch it on the way back if RE goes beyond symmetrical: right click; inside)

  6. After three stair-step brackets in a row on a Trend Day, expect the fourth to continue the trend.
