Clean Execution

  • Limit Buy below (Blue), Limit Sell above (Red)

Pre Market

  1. Set MP Time Template to 24 Hour
  2. Clear Triggered Alert List
  3. Set up EFS functions
    1. Line and Labels
      1. overnight high & low
      2. previous day's high + 2.5/low - 2.5
    2. Opening Range and VA Entry … previous day’s VAH, VAL & POC (must be a 1-minute chart)
    3. Weekly Pivots
    4. MP Periods
    5. DVA Tool
    6. Prev OHLC
  4. Find out if there’s any news
  5. Anticipate setups for opening In or Out Of Value based on overnight trading

At the Open

In Value Out Of Value
Short VAH
  •  3 points or more away: prepare for OR Trade
  • Closer than 3 points: short VAL/buy VAH
     
  • Look for +/- 2.5
Long VAL
Second time through, look at POC
PLAN ahead for what to do if stopped
  • If it pushes away from you, where might it turn around?
  • If it pushes strongly toward you, where might it stop?

At 9:55 AM … NEWS

  1. If there’s NEWS, get off your trades
  2. Set MP Time Template to Future
  3. Turn on EFS functions
    1. Daily HiLoPPB (toggle PPB lines)
    2. Initial Balance (toggle the 1-tick announcement)
    3. Daily Pivot (toggle Dentist lines)
    4. Range Extension (requires daily hi/lo and IB)
  4. Plan post-IB setups
